Our impact

Our goal at WhereNext Experiences is to provide travel experiences that support long term sustainability within the communities that we visit.

The suppliers we work with are carefully selected to ensure environmental and animal protection. Each experience we provide details the impact to local communities and the environment.

Tourism is a fundamental part of many developing countries and has been decimated in the pandemic. There are a number of charities and organisations that we support to further these initiatives.

Pledge 1% is a corporate philanthropy movement dedicated to making the community a key stakeholder in every business. Pledge 1% encourages and challenges individuals and companies to pledge 1% of equity, product, and employee time for their communities.

Our story

I was working at Salesforce.com who provides seven days of volunteer time off as part of their commitment to the Pledge 1% organisation. These volunteer days can be used for various local or global initiatives and we have been involved in many different projects including beach clean ups, food banks and helping disadvantaged children.

Whilst working in the Singapore office a group of us went to Nepal in 2019 to renovate a sanctuary for disadvantaged children affected by human trafficking between Nepal and India. Before the trip to Nepal we also raised around US$50k to donate to NGO’s in the region.

Another memorable experience was volunteering with the Pandas at the Bifengxia Giant Panda Base in Bifengxia Town, Ya'an, Sichuan, China.

This bucket list experience provided funds for Panda research and local economic development. It was an unforgettable experience in rural China working with these beautiful creatures.

The idea was born to combine my love of travel with animal conservation and sustainable tourism and build a values based company taking inspiration from Salesforce.com and other companies such as Patagonia that prove that companies can provide a positive impact in the world.
